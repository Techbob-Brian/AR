Links needed:
1. 虛擬角色工具 https://vroid.com/en/studio
2. 虛擬角色動畫 https://www.mixamo.com/

Steps to bring your character to Unity:
1. Install VRoid studio using Link 1.
2. Download your final product in VRoid studio as .vrm file
3. Open Unity and import from UniVrm file
4. import your .vrm by the tool bar on top

Steps to add animatio to your character:
1. Go to Link 2 and pick animations.
2. Download it as .fbx file.
